Choosing the right laptop repair service in Nairobi

If you are comparing local repair options, start by identifying whether the problem is physical, electrical, or performance-related. Cracked displays and broken keyboards usually need replacement parts, while slow laptops often improve with SSD upgrades, RAM upgrades, or software cleanup.

Customers searching for motherboard repair in Nairobi or laptop not turning on repair should begin with diagnosis first, because power faults can come from several causes and not all of them need the same solution.

When to repair, upgrade, or replace a laptop

Repair is often the best move when the issue is isolated, like a cracked screen, bad battery, or failed keyboard. An upgrade is often smarter when the laptop is slow but still structurally sound. Replacement is the better route when the total repair cost no longer makes sense.
